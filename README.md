# DoorDash Customer & Sales Analytics Using Excel

## Overview
This project analyzes DoorDash customer data using Microsoft Excel to uncover spending behavior, income distribution, and campaign performance insights. The goal is to understand how customer income and marketing campaigns influence total spending.

## Dataset
- 2,205 customers (rows)
- 36 attributes (columns)
- Each row represents one unique customer

## Tools & Techniques
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Cleaning & Validation
- Pivot Tables
- Histogram & Scatter Plot
- VLOOKUP & XLOOKUP
- SUM, IF, SUMIF, COUNTIF

## Key Insights
- **Campaign 6** represents ~15% of customers (311) but generates **~25% of total revenue**
- Campaign 6 customers spend **~62.7% more per customer** than the overall average
- **Income vs Spending:** Strong positive correlation  
  - R² = **0.6774** (Income explains ~68% of spending variation)
- Histogram shows **mid-income customers ($31,993–$80,978)** are the core spending group

## Conclusion
Campaign 6 is a high-value customer segment with strong monetization efficiency. Income is a major driver of customer spending, and mid-income users present the best opportunity for targeted, high-ROI marketing strategies.
